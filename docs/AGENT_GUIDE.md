# Agent Guide

## Overview
This document outlines the three primary types of agents used in the StockAI platform, detailing their decision-making processes, personas, character profiles, bias profiles, trading logic, and performance tracking mechanisms.

---

## Agent Types

### 1. Market Maker Agent
- **Decision-Making Process:** 
  - Utilizes algorithms to predict market trends based on real-time data. 
  - Identifies arbitrage opportunities by analyzing price discrepancies across different exchanges.
- **Persona:**  
  - Analytical and risk-averse, focused on liquidity and stability.
- **Character Profile:** 
  - Logical, data-driven, prioritizes long-term growth over short-term gains.
- **Bias Profile:** 
  - May exhibit conservative bias due to a focus on minimizing risk.
- **Trading Logic:** 
  - Engages in high-frequency trading, ensuring continuous market presence to avoid large price fluctuations.
- **Performance Tracking:** 
  - Monitored through metrics like average trade duration, profit margins, and liquidity provided.

### 2. Swing Trader Agent
- **Decision-Making Process:** 
  - Analyzes medium-term market trends and identifies stocks with potential for short to medium-term profit. 
  - Makes decisions based on technical analysis and market sentiment.
- **Persona:**  
  - Patient and opportunistic, looking for the best entry and exit points.
- **Character Profile:** 
  - Balanced approach, can be both analytical and intuitive.
- **Bias Profile:** 
  - Tendency towards confirmation bias; may favor information that supports existing beliefs.
- **Trading Logic:** 
  - Utilizes chart patterns and indicators, focusing on buying low and selling high.
- **Performance Tracking:** 
  - Assessed based on return on investment (ROI) and the success rate of trades.

### 3. Arbitrage Agent
- **Decision-Making Process:** 
  - Continuously scans multiple exchanges for price differences to exploit for profit. 
  - Executes trades quickly to capitalize on fleeting opportunities.
- **Persona:**  
  - Fast-paced and opportunistic, thrives on volatility.
- **Character Profile:** 
  - Quick decision-maker, embraces risk for potential high rewards.
- **Bias Profile:** 
  - Risk-taking bias; often underestimates potential losses in favor of high-return opportunities.
- **Trading Logic:** 
  - Focuses on executing simultaneous buy/sell operations across different platforms to lock in profit.
- **Performance Tracking:** 
  - Performance is measured through success ratio of arbitrage trades and average profit per trade.

---

## Conclusion
Understanding the distinct characteristics and strategies of each agent type is critical for optimizing the performance of the StockAI platform. By aligning trading strategies with specific market conditions and agent strengths, the overall trading efficacy can be significantly enhanced.
